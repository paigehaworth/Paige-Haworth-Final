Publication Design

Since my freshman year of high school, I have been working on my high school's literary and art magazine, Menagerie. Being on the art staff, I work hands-on with Adobe InDesign to design the layout of the pages and decide what art goes with the literary pieces. By junior year I held the position as Art Editor. As COVID-19 took over and school went fully remote, I ended up working with 3 other people to complete the whole book. Together, we designed over 70 pages from home and worked together to produce the best book we could while in a whole new environment. As a senior, I was the Editor-in-Chief of the magazine and I was able to lead a creative staff to put out a well rounded magazine.
